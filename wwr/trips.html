Plan your trips TODAY!

There are many ways to enjoy a river trip. Here, we have different options on how to plan it, split into 3 difficulties. First, we have the easy path. One set for family with smaller children, safe but still enjoyable. Second, the experienced path, medium. This course consists of rapids that will swing you around. Great for most people. And finally, we have the hard path, consisting of rapids that could potentially knock your boat over, if you are not careful. Each of these paths hold any option to your liking, and are fit for people of all ages. Just remember to have fun!
